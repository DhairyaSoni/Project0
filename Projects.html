  • Simple bank web application

    This project is a small example of the bank website. For this project, I have used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery for the fornt end and PHP for back end. SQL is also playing majore role for this project.

    When the user enter to this application, a Log In page appears. If the user is new then there is also an option to create new account. He can create his account by clicking on Create Account, and he can enter his password and how much amount he like in his Saving and in his Check In account. He will get fix amount for the Credit Card. Moreover, he can not choose or change his Account Number.

    There is also an option on Log in page, if user forgot his password. If he clicks on that option, he will reach to the Forget Password page, which ask about his Account Number. After entering the account number, his email will be apear and he will get an email, which has his password and also some more information like time and date, in the email body.

    In this application, user can add the Pay Check, he even can use the amount of money from different banking account.
